Who We Are

Pixel Tees By Powell is a self-love-focused fashion brand that blends retro pixel design with modern messages of confidence. Every tee is crafted to make you feel proud, powerful, and seen.

Our Mission

We’re here to help you love yourself out loud. Our mission is to bring unique, pixel-art-inspired clothing to people of all ages and backgrounds — making fashion fun, inclusive, and uplifting.
